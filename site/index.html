About Bree

A Nevada native, Bree has walked the shamanic path of timelessness since childhood. Compounded with years of spiritual experiences as well as formal training with The Four Winds' shamanic teacher and medical anthropologist Dr. Alberto Villoldo, Bree's initiation with the medicine wheel and ancestors has awoken shamanic sight and intentionality which she shares readily in session.

Bree helps clients find their sacred synchronicity to the greater whole, feel lighter, find peace, release trauma, disengage blocking frequencies, and remove stale qi (chi).

Bree's passion and connection to nature extends to her green thumb as well. She plants, nurtures, grows and harvests organic herbal plants that she converts to medicinal teas, tinctures, topicals and more.

Formally trained with Dr. Alberto Villoldo at The Four Winds — the world's leading training program in shamanic energy medicine.

The Practice

Understanding shamanic energy medicine

The Luminous Energy Field

Creation happens at four different levels. Each level aligns with a direction of the medicine wheel: the body, the mind, the soul, and the spirit. Western medicine works at the level of the body. Shamans work at the level of spirit — with pure energy and the Luminous Energy Field.

Your Luminous Energy Field contains information and instructions on how your body is built, as well as how your emotions, actions and behaviors will manifest. Unresolved traumas leave imprints that, if not confronted, manifest as illness, negative patterns, and being stuck in fight-or-flight mode.

The illumination process cleanses these imprints so you can heal from the past. Your culture, your parents, your wounds, your stories no longer define you — and you become the author of your own destiny.

This work requires discipline, humility, integrity and living life impeccably. Love is the operating principle in life.

After your initial shamanic session, Bree will determine if a custom tea blend would be beneficial for your continued healing. The herbs are prepared, weighed, packaged and labeled — ready for pick-up at your next appointment.
